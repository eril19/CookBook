Quaker du lezato

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of old-fashioned rolled oats (such as Quaker Oats Old-Fashioned Rolled Oats)
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 1/2 cups of Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cinnamon
  • 1 1/4 cup Honey
  • 1/4 Kosher Salt
  • 2 spoon of Yogurt

    Instruction

  • Combine oats, milk, water, salt, and cinnamon in a
    medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce
    heat to low.
  • Simmer uncovered for 3 to 5 minutes until
    thickened, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat
    and let cool slightly.
  • Divide equally between two bowls. Drizzle each
    serving with 1/2 teaspoon honey. Add additional
    desired toppings and serve.

    Notes

  • For dairy-free oatmeal, substitute your favorite nut milk.
